1. Information we collect

When a customer asks for a rental, TETAWEN CARS may collect identification details, contact details, rental dates, driving licence information and information needed to prepare a rental contract.

2. How information is used

Information is used to answer requests, manage reservations, prepare rental documents, verify eligibility, provide customer support and comply with applicable business, accounting and legal requirements.

3. Sharing of information

TETAWEN CARS does not sell customer personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary with service providers, insurers, legal advisers, public authorities or other parties required for rental management and compliance.

4. Retention

Rental and identity records may be retained for the period needed to provide services, resolve disputes, satisfy accounting obligations and meet legal retention duties.

5. Security

TETAWEN CARS uses reasonable administrative and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss or misuse.

6. Customer rights

Customers may contact TETAWEN CARS to request access, correction or deletion of personal information where applicable and subject to legal retention requirements.
